E-Hospital System

Welcome to E-Hospital System

This E-Hospital System is a centralized platform designed to facilitate the seamless interaction between patients,

doctors, lab administrators, pharmacy, and billing staff. The goal is to ensure faster services, less paperwork,

and enhanced communication across departments.

  • Patients can check doctor availability and book appointments.
  • Doctors can view, accept, or reject appointments and provide digital prescriptions.
  • Lab Admins update lab test results directly into the system.
  • Pharmacists manage medicine stock and deliveries.
  • Billing Staff generate invoices based on patient activities.
  • Reports section offers doctor availability, patient visits, and pharmacy deliveries.
